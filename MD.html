You are Welcome to My Multimedia/Hypermedia Page

Multimedia technology helps create an interactive content for both in-person and virtual classroom environments. It encompasses a variety of technological tools including those that create audio, audiovisual, and visual tools for effective learning and teaching outcomes. Development of the appropriate technologies or multimedia tools are within the remit of Technology coaches. These coaches help other stakeholders to design and develop multimedia platforms that meet standards including ethical and philosophical standards.
