You are Welcome to My Leadership Page

Leadership inspires, participate, and galvanizes the necessary support to comprehensively develop and integrate technology in the instructional process to achieve effective teaching and learning outcomes. In other words, leadership facilitates the transformational mechanisms that feature contemporary technology in the instructional process whilst maintaining the core objectives of education and the philosophies of education.

This page contains my course and field artifacts that demonstrate various activities that reflect the leadership competencies and skills I have acquired in the program.
